What is this substance? About squalene Squalene is a naturally occurring hydrocarbon, that is, a molecule made mostly of carbon and hydrogen. It's found in high concentrations in the livers of sharks, but it's also present in smaller amounts in other animals, including humans. It's a key component of human sebum, an oily substance produced by our skin glands.
