1. Coconut

Coconut has several benefits for your hair. Not only does it promote hair growth, it also conditions it.

It has essential fats, minerals, and proteins which reduce hair breakage and is also rich in potassium and iron. You can also make use of coconut oil or milk to prevent hair fall.

How to prevent hair loss with coconut?

Warm a bit of coconut oil and massage it from your hair root to tip and wash after one hour.
