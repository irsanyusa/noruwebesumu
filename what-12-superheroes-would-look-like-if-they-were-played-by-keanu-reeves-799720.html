What 12 Superheroes Would Look Like If They Were Played by Keanu Reeves

In 2019, a group of people collected hundreds of thousands of signatures for Keanu Reeves to be declared the “person of the year.” Although they did not reach the required total, Reeves remains a reference in countless discussions. We even produced an article about the 10 very good reasons why everyone falls in love with him (well, there are more than 1,000). In there, we highlighted Reeves’ principles, his charity work, his respect for people, his kindness, and his humility. Without a doubt, he is one of the most popular actors in Hollywood and that’s why we want to pay special tribute to him. Because, after all, it seems that Keanu Reeves can make anything happen — the guy has it all. So why not picture him as a superhero? He’s already is one in real life anyway...

Bright Side has an amazing team of illustrators and graphic designers that helped us with this task. By the way, most of them love Keanu Reeves too, so it’s not like it was all that hard to carry out the assignment. Our favorite image is Reeves as Wolverine.
