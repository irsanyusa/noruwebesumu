Brigitte Bardot (Actress and Model)

Profession: Actress and Model

Biography: Known simply as "B.B.", Bardot was one of the best-known sex symbols of the 1950s and 1960s. She first achieved fame in the 1957 film And God Created Woman.

Before she retired in 1973, Bardot appeared in the 1963 film Le Mépris and in the 1965 film Viva Maria!.

Born: September 28, 1934
Birthplace: Paris, France
Age: 89 years old
