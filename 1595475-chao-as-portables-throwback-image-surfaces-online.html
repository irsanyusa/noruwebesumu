Online Frenzy as Portable's Throwback Image Surfaces, Evokes Reactions: &quot;He Was Healthy&quot;

  • A throwback picture of a street singer, Habeeb Okikiola Badmus, professionally known as Portable Zazu, has sparked reactions online
  • Fans gave different opinions about the old visual images of the singer posted on an Instagram blog
  • According to some, he looked way better at the time than he does now, and they blamed it on his dramatic public display

Habeeb Okikiola Badmus, a Nigerian street singer known as Portable, is trending online after an old picture of him surfaced.

Portable is known for his unique look. The singer's hair is dyed in different colours, and he expresses himself clearly through fashion.

However, pictures from when he seemed younger and in a very different form have emerged online.

How Portable looked in a throwback photo

In the said picture, Portable looked calm and young. His hair was dark, and his style was not as controversial as it is.
