America’s Got Talent Season 11 star Caly Bevier recently announced that she’s releasing a new single called “Say You’re Mine” on Valentine’s Day. This is the singer’s first new music since she appeared on AGT: All-Stars earlier this year.

AGT Star Caly Bevier Teases New Single ‘Say You’re Mine’

Earlier this week, Bevier took to Instagram to tell followers that “Say You’re Mine” will be released on Valentine’s Day, February 14. She’ll be releasing the song through the W3 platform Sound.xyz. Bevier posted a colorful moving image to go with the song’s teaser.

She wrote in the caption that “this specific art work will only be available to the people who collect it,” adding, “I made the art myself and would love for you to snag 1 when the song drops. There will only be 44 available!”

The post also includes a handwritten note from Bevier to someone special, as she writes, “I wrote a song for you. When we’re apart I can’t get you out of my head. It’s almost as if I can feel your heartbeat from miles away.” The singer also included a photo of herself and her husband embracing on a beach.

The Singer Recently Competed on All-Stars

Earlier this year, Bevier returned to the AGT stage to compete on the spin-off All-Stars. The singer originally made it to the Semifinals of Season 11, when she was 16 years old. This time, she returned for a chance to be named the best of the best.

For her preliminary performance, Bevier performed an original song called “Head Held High.” She earned a standing ovation from all three judges. Bevier ended up being in the Top 3 for the night according to the Superfan vote. Unfortunately, she didn’t move on to the Finals.

Since her first AGT appearance, Bevier has released a number of original singles, available to stream now. Are you excited to hear “Say You’re Mine” when it’s released next week? Let us know in the comments below.
