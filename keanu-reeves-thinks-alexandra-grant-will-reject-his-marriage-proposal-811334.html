Keanu Reeves Thinks Alexandra Grant Will Reject His Marriage Proposal

A love connection between Keanu Reeves and Alexandra Grant didn’t start right away. Their fans have followed them for a long time, and there is some hope that there will be a wedding.

Avalon/Photoshot/East News, Avalon/Photoshot/East News

Reeves and Grant met at a dinner party in 2009, and first, it started as a friendship and professional interest. The pair collaborated on several projects like Reeve’s books Ode to Happiness and Shadows.

In November 2019, it was officially reported that they were dating after their first red carpet debut together. Actually, a source said that at the time that they had “been dating for years” and that “it wasn’t a secret.”

Invision/Invision/East News

Recently, it’s been reported that Keanu Reeves wants to propose. But there is one thing. Keanu is afraid to do it because he thinks that Grant might say no.

Elizabeth Goodenough/Everett Collection/East News

“He has the ring and the proposal speech all ready, but just can’t follow through. He also worries about jinxing something that’s already so perfect.”

The insider shared, “He just needs to build up his confidence — and shake those jitters.”
