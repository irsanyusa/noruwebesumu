Voting Intention: Con 21%, Lab 40% (26-27 Mar 2024)

Latest YouGov Westminster voting intention figures

The latest YouGov/Times voting intention poll has the Conservatives on 21% (+2 from 19-20 March) while Labour's vote share has dropped four points to 40%.

For the second week in a row we find Reform UK polling at a new highest ever figure, this time at 16% (+1).

Elsewhere, the Lib Dems are on 10% (+1) and the Greens are on 8% (no change).

When it comes to who Britons think would make the best prime minister, 31% opt for Keir Starmer (-2 from 14-15 Feb), while only 18% say the same of Rishi Sunak (also -2). More than four in ten (43%) are unsure.
