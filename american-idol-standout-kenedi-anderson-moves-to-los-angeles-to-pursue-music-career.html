'American Idol' Standout Kenedi Anderson Moves to Los Angeles to Pursue Music Career

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

Photo by ABC/Eric McCandless

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Kenedi Anderson, the 19-year-old singer who withdrew from American Idol in 2022, has announced her recent move to Los Angeles, California, to pursue her music career. Anderson shared the news on her Instagram account, where she posted a photo of herself standing in front of the iconic Hollywood sign.

Kenedi Anderson Announced Her Move to Los Angeles

Anderson first gained recognition when she competed on season 20 of American Idol in 2022. She wowed all three judges and earned a golden ticket to Hollywood. After making it to the top ten, Anderson decided to leave the show for personal reasons.

Anderson’s move to LA is a major step in her music career. The city is home to the music industry’s biggest record labels, publishers, and management companies. It is also a hotbed of creativity and opportunity.

Anderson is no stranger to success. She was a fan favorite on American Idol and was praised by the judges for her powerful voice and her ability to sing a variety of genres. She is also a talented songwriter and has written her own songs since she was a child.

Anderson’s move is an inspiring story of resilience and determination. Despite leaving American Idol early, she never gave up on her dreams and is now living them out in LA. It just goes to show that anything is possible if you put your mind to it.

What Happened to Kenedi Anderson After Leaving American Idol?

Although she departed for personal reasons, Anderson has continued to showcase her vocal prowess by frequently sharing singing videos on her TikTok page, where she has amassed an impressive following of nearly 500,000 people.

In addition to her online presence, Anderson achieved a significant milestone by completing her high school education and graduating in June 2022, as announced on her Instagram account.

Notably, in September 2022, Anderson collaborated with fellow former American Idol contestants Emyrson Flora and Paige Fish for a live performance in Provo, Utah. The trio showcased their combined talents, delighting the audience with their captivating performances.

Anderson also revealed her future plans to Our Era Magazine, expressing her excitement about releasing new music. She has been actively involved in songwriting and believes that some of her best compositions are yet to be unveiled.
