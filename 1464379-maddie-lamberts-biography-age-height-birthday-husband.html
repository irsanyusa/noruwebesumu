In those videos and other vlogs Maddie has shared on her channel, she has talked about her relationship with her baby daddy and some major events of her life.

The YouTuber shared about her parents’ divorce, her first serious relationship, dealing with teen pregnancy, the birth of her daughter, and overcoming depression caused by bullying at school, online trolls, and fake friends.
