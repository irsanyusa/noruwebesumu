Dallas Stars in History - On This Day

Events in Sport

  • 1995-12-11 Thomas O Hicks buys NHL Dallas Stars for $84 million
  • 1998-02-07 NHL's Dallas Stars retire Neal Broten's #7
  • 1998-06-05 NHL Western Conference Final: Detroit Red Wings beat Dallas Stars, 4 games to 2
  • 1999-06-04 NHL Western Conference Final: Dallas Stars beat Colorado Avalanche, 4 games to 3

Stanley Cup

1999-06-19 Stanley Cup, Marine Midland Arena, Buffalo, NY: Dallas Stars edge Buffalo Sabres, 2-1 in triple overtime for 4-2 series win; Brett Hull clinches series with infamous "No Goal" ruling

  • 2000-05-27 NHL Western Conference Final: Dallas Stars beat Colorado Avalanche, 4 games to 3
  • 2000-06-10 Stanley Cup Final, Reunion Arena, Dallas, TX: New Jersey Devils defeat Dallas Stars, 2-1 in double OT for a 4-2 series victory
  • 2008-05-19 NHL Western Conference Final: Detroit Red Wings beat Dallas Stars, 4 games to 2
  • 2017-10-06 Vegas Golden Knights make their NHL debut in Dallas, winning 2-1 over the Stars
  • 2020-09-28 Stanley Cup Final, Rogers Place, Edmonton, Alberta: Tampa Bay Lightning claim second championship in franchise history with a 2-0 Game 6 win over Dallas Stars; win series 4-2
