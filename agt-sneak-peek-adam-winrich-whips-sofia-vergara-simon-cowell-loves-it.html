Sofia Vergara will take the stage like never before on America’s Got Talent auditions six. You don’t want to miss this exhilarating whip cracking audition!

In this leaked America’s Got Talent audition, whipcracker Adam Winrich takes the stage. Though it is not shown in this video, it’s implied that the Wisconsin native asks Vergara to join him on stage. The act features Adam cracking his whips around the actress. The act intensifies when he gets closer and closer to Vergara.

Even though Vergara looks terrified, it seems like Simon Cowell, Heidi Klum, and Terry Crews are loving it. The two judges are seen clapping for Adam, while Cowell asks for more. The audience is also roaring for this unique act. I can’t wait to see what the judges say about this act!

Who is Adam Winrich?

From this leaked clip, it’s clear that Adam is a seasoned whip performer. This whipcracker is actually a 31-time Guiness World Record Holder. His records include “Most Candles Extinguished with a Whip in one minute, “Loudest Whip Crack,” “Most Drink Cans Broken with a Whip in Three Minutes,” and more. This whipcracker makes a living performing fire whip at festivals across the United States.

Adam is no stranger to national television. He’s reportedly appeared on several television shows including Conan O’Brien, Steve Harvey, and The Discovery Channel. Ahead of America’s Got Talent season 17, he has tried out in 2012 and a few years after. His previous auditions did not air.

Adam has a relatively large following on his social media profiles. He currently has 37,500 followers on Instagram, 19,000 followers on Facebook, and 42,300 subscribers on YouTube. Outside of his whip cracking, Adam posts videos of him playing the harmonica on YouTube. He’s an impressive harmonica player, so we hope to see him incorporate this into his America’s Got Talent act in the future.

Be sure to tune into America’s Got Talent on Tuesday, July 12 to see if Adam makes it into the next round of the competition.
