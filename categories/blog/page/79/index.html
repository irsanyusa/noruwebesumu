published on August 17, 2024
23. 2. 2020 | News Ninety-eight musicians from 23 countries will compete in the Prague Spring competition of young players that annually precedes the Prague Spring international music festival in May. This year, the contest will focus on the bassoon and clarinet categories. The three-round contest, which is a part of the Prague Spring festival, will take place from May 8 to May 14. All the rounds are open to the public; the final rounds traditionally attract greatest interest, where competitors are accompanied by a symphony orchestra in the Rudolfinum’s Dvořák Hall.
