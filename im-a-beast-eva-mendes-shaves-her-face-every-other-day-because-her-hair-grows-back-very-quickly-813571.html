Im a Beast, Eva Mendes Shaves Her Face Every Other Day Because Her Hair Grows Back Ve

Eva Mendes isn’t scared to talk about dealing with annoying facial hair. The actress revealed her secret beauty trick for getting radiant skin on Instagram. In two separate posts, Eva opened up about dermaplaning treatments. It’s a fancy skincare technique where you shave off the top layer of skin and hair on your face.

Her hair grows back very quickly.

The star doesn’t hide the fact that she’s hairy, “My Cuban Papi is a man bear, and I’m his mini me. I’m a beast, so I probably need to shave every other day! My hair grows back if I get chills. I’ll literally be shaving my legs in the shower and get chills and all my work is undone!”

It’s the secret to glowing skin.

Someone asked Eva if she noticed the difference after the treatment, and she replied, “Yes. Makeup and moisturizer go on much smoother.” Later the star shared a photo showing off her glowing and smooth skin after the procedure.

Many of her female followers do that too.

Eva was happy to connect with her Instagram audience, and she even replied to her fans’ comments. The star found out there were lots of women who shaved their faces too. Mendez followed up with another post saying, “I received a lot of comments on yesterday’s post from women who’ve been shaving their face for years-ok I guess ’dermaplaning’ is the preferred word, but it is what it is, and I LOVE it!”

Dermaplaning doesn’t cause stubble.

Dermaplaning won’t magically make your hair grow thicker or darker, or leave you with a pesky stubble. The hair might feel slightly different as it starts growing back due to the way it was shaved off, but in reality, it’s still the same thickness and texture as before, and it will keep growing at the same old pace. So, no need to worry about any wild hair rumors. Besides, it also makes your skin brighter and softer, just like Eva’s!

Eva isn’t the only celebrity who loves to share beauty tips. Heidi Klum also shared some tricks (which are affordable) that enhance her natural beauty.
